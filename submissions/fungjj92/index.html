John Donut Apple Fritter

A well-loved institution of St. Louis, MO whose very name conjures diabetes and epic Harold and Kumar-esque pilgrimages when the clock strikes 11PM, Sun - Fri. The hot oven-fresh apple fritter donut tastes even better against the backdrop of Mr. John Donut's sassy remarks.

Jay's Greek Salad

Fresh made blocks of feta cheese are sold at rock bottom prices at Jay's International Grocery Store, food mecca for all cultures of people who live in the surrounding St. Louis, MO neighborhoods. Chef = yours truly. Satisfaction levels reached another level after a week of emotional buildup for the complex creamy fresh goodness.

Fries and French Toast Milkshake

Imagine slurping a milkshake where you can feel the maple syrup swirled perfectly into sweet goodness complete with french toast and cone pieces throughout. Then dip perfect hot crispy fries into it. Weep. Repeat. Bailey's Range, St. Louis, MO.

Xi'an Buckwheat Noodles

Homage to Muslim-influenced Western Chinese cuisine from Xi'an province, Xi'an Famous Foods in NYC makes your mind and body crave noodles. Handmade using buckwheat and several choices of spicy sauces, the chewy "Q texture" and flavors are both one-of-a-kind and wholly addictive. A favorite of food mogul Anthony Bourdain.

Crawfish Nachos

Only 1 known person, after over 100 miles of cycling, finished an order of these babies from Not Yo Mama's Kitchen in New Roads, Louisiana. Then, there is me and my girlfriends on a road trip. Each topping was cooked to stand alone in utter delectiblity. When combined, sent fireworks into the sky and ladies under the table. Never underestimate gas station restaurants in rural America.

Ceviche de Pulpo

Picture it: A quiet, open-aired, family owned eatery straddling lapping waves and the sandy shore in a modest fishing town, Puerto Lopez. At daybreak, you watched the boats come to shore, expert fishermen haul in the night's catch, and fill the beach with scavenging birds, scales, and the blood of butchered marlins. At sunset you eat the freshest and most perfectly balanced flavors and textures you have ever known and most of the world will never know. This is octopus ceviche con pasta de maní. Simplemente magnífico.

Sitting in a puddle of your owl drool? Time to eat something tasty.
